About Us

Our Approach

We believe everyone in the legal community should have a clean, vibrant, sustainable environment to work in.  We strive to build the best spaces in the greatest locations to serve your office and staff needs. We are quickly branching out into the cannabis arena by working to provide clean affordable grow locations throughout the entire Western States region.  Our current facilities technologies are tailored to integrate indoor grows alongside existing businesses.  This tech revolves around odor management to reduce or even eliminate all grow related emissions.

Our Story

Seeing a lack of affordable and sustainable office space needs for young budding attorneys, David Briar decided it was time to do something about it.  His vision it to use sustainable and green technologies to build great office spaces that appeal to those attorney professionals just starting their careers. David is excited about moving into the cannabis business and is looking forward to creating exciting and efficient growing facilities for newly created cannabis growers.
